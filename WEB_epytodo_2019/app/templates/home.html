Sometimes we all procrastinate or end our days feeling like we haven't accomplished anything at all. One might think that it is a problem of laziness. In reality it is more often due to an organizational problem. When you have a lot of tasks to do and you don't organize them in order of priority, you are more likely to procrastinate because you create an additional difficulty. Likewise, when we don't define in advance what we have to work on during the day, we start working on a bit of everything and anything. And we usually end our day dissatisfied with our work because we are scattered. To avoid this, we need to better organize and plan our days. In this article we will therefore see how to organize yourself every day to be more efficient.
